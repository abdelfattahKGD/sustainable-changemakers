About SDG Classroom

The SDG Classroom is an initiative within the NASA Space Apps Challenge designed to teach students about the importance of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). By focusing on real-world challenges, students will develop innovative solutions to global issues.

We aim to empower the younger generation by offering interactive educational tools that incorporate space data and technologies to understand and solve complex global problems. Join us as we work together to make a sustainable future!

Goals

  • Increase awareness about the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
  • Inspire young minds to explore innovative solutions using space technology.
  • Empower students to think critically about global environmental challenges.
  • Collaborate with space experts to develop educational materials that are impactful and engaging.
